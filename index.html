About Hay & Wilson

Serving Northern Nevada - We are a licensed electrical contracting firm specializing in commercial and residential electrical infrastructure. Our expertise encompasses cutting-edge technologies including data center systems, renewable energy integration, and mission-critical power solutions.

With over two decades of combined experience in electrical contracting, our team has established a reputation for technical excellence throughout the Reno metropolitan area. We have successfully delivered electrical infrastructure for advanced data centers, contributing to research and development facilities that demand the highest standards of precision and reliability.

Our specialization in battery backup systems and energy storage solutions positions us at the forefront of sustainable power infrastructure. We design and implement large-scale battery installations for data centers and commercial facilities, ensuring uninterrupted operations during grid fluctuations. As electric vehicle infrastructure expands across the region, we have developed comprehensive capabilities in EV charging systems, from residential Level 2 installations to commercial DC fast-charging networks.

Our technical proficiency extends to complex electrical wiring and diagnostic troubleshooting, with a documented track record of resolving sophisticated electrical challenges in both legacy and contemporary systems. From new construction electrical design to retrofitting existing structures with advanced electrical infrastructure, our team delivers engineered solutions with meticulous attention to code compliance and safety protocols. Whether deploying AI/ML computing infrastructure, integrating renewable energy systems, or upgrading electrical distribution panels, we maintain adherence to the highest industry standards.

Committed to quality, safety, and innovation, we provide comprehensive electrical engineering solutions for commercial enterprises and residential properties throughout the Reno area. Our combination of technical expertise, regulatory compliance, and regional experience ensures successful project execution from initial consultation through final commissioning.
